EDI Standards
📋 Supported EDI Standards & Transaction Sets
✦ ANSI X12 ✦ UN/EDIFACT ✦ JSON/REST ✦ XML / cXML
840 Purchase Requisition X12 · REQOTE (EDIFACT) Internal PR raised by requestor. Routes into approval workflow before PO generation.
850 Purchase Order X12 · ORDERS (EDIFACT) Standard PO sent to supplier. Supports Blanket, Planned & Contract PO variants.
855 / 865 PO Acknowledgement X12 · ORDRSP (EDIFACT) Supplier confirms or changes acceptance of PO terms and line items.
856 Ship Notice (ASN) X12 · DESADV (EDIFACT) Advance Shipping Notice updates WMS & receiving dock with shipment details.
810 Invoice X12 · INVOIC (EDIFACT) Supplier invoice → AP matching. 2-way, 3-way & 4-way match supported.
875 / 870 Sales Order X12 · ORDERS (EDIFACT) Inbound customer orders from buyers. Triggers OMS / ERP order creation.
997 Functional ACK X12 · CONTRL (EDIFACT) Auto-generated acknowledgement confirming receipt & syntax validity of EDI set.

REMADV EDIFACT Messages UN/EDIFACT D96A / D01B ORDERS, ORDRSP, DESADV, INVOIC, REMADV, CONTRL natively parsed & generated.
